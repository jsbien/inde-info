Search can be limited to a specific volume in two ways: using sections, e.g. within vol1,..., within vol6, within vol6part1 or within vol6part2, or using metadata, e.g. meta volume=6 .

The within clause can be also used to limit the search to the following sections: body, errata, intro, info, varia, front, back.

{% endblocktrans %} {% blocktrans %}
